Faq - Regarding Online Verification of Certificate

You can apply for the Online Verification of Certificate on the Punjab School Education Board website https://pseb.ac.in/ through the link given below. Please follow link: -‘Home-> Online Forms-> Apply for Second Copy of Certificate-> Apply Online Verification of Certificate'.

Please follow link((https://www.pseb.ac.in/) ‘Home-> Online Forms-> Apply For Second Copy of Certificate-> Apply For Online Verification Of Certificate' for getting your fee details.

Please follow the link(https://www.pseb.ac.in/) ‘Home-> Online Forms-> Apply For Second Copy of Certificate-> Apply-> Second Copy of Certificate->Check Application Status'. You can find your application status with Registration Number or Case Number or Roll Number.
